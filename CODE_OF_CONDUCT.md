# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want Mica to be a respectful, constructive project where people can collaborate safely across different experience levels and perspectives.

Examples of positive behavior:

- being respectful in disagreement
- giving specific, actionable feedback
- assuming good intent while staying honest about problems
- helping newer contributors get oriented

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption of collaboration spaces

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers privately through the channel they designate for the repository.
