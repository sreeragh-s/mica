# notelab.io — Landing page marketing copy

**Tone:** Fun, human, a little cheeky—still honest about what the app does.

notelab is a **desktop** notes app for people who **write**, **doodle**, **Git-push** when they feel like it, and sometimes **ask their past self** a question—with **AI that actually reads *your* notes**, not random internet opinions.

**The privacy bit (plain English):** We’re **not** building a giant filing cabinet in the sky for your stuff. Your notes—and the bits that power smart search and chat—**aren’t parked in our database** as “the master copy.” Your brain dump stays **yours**.

## FAQ-ready Q&A (still SEO-friendly, less robot)

**What is notelab?**  
A desktop app to **write** rich notes, **draw** with Excalidraw, **organize** workspaces, **search** fast, **see** links as a pretty graph, and optionally **chat** with AI that pulls from what *you* wrote.

**Is my stuff in the cloud?**  
Your notes live **on your machine** first. If you use Git remotes, that’s **your** call. **We don’t use a database on our side to store your data—including the AI-related bits**—so we’re not the “single source of truth” for your vault.

**Do you keep my AI helper data in your database?**  
**Nope.** That category of data **isn’t** sitting in a **notelab user database**. It’s treated as **yours**, **local-first**—not our souvenir collection.

**Do I need GitHub?**  
You can use a lot without it. Some connected features may ask you to sign in—check the app for what’s current.

**Can I use it offline?**  
Writing, drawing, and Git work great locally. Anything that phones home for AI needs a connection when you use it.

**Why not just use a website?**  
Because sometimes you want a **real app**—dock icon, shortcuts, fewer “oops I closed the tab” tragedies.

## Brand voice notes (keep copy consistent)

- **Fun, not flippant:** Jokes are fine; don’t bury what the product actually does.  
- **Plain English first:** Say “smart search and chat” before acronyms; explain nerdy bits only where trust demands it.  
- **Privacy without fear-mongering:** “We’re not hoarding your stuff” beats a lecture on architecture.  
- **Honest about AI:** Helpful, not magic; optional; tied to your content when you turn it on.  
- **Verbs with energy:** Write, sketch, link, search, commit, ask, laugh once in a while.
